About SmartVille

Building Better Communities

A housing-focused company based in Segamat, committed to strengthening community relationships and promoting better organization within housing areas.

Our Mission

To provide a centralized, structured platform where residents can connect, organize, and celebrate community life seamlessly.

Our Vision

A trusted and reputable housing company expanding across Malaysia, setting the standard for smart, connected living environments.

Our Values

Innovation, efficiency, reliability, and above all â€” community well-being and social harmony at the heart of everything we do.

The Problem

  • Residents rely on fragmented WhatsApp/word-of-mouth
  • Missed event information & poor coordination
  • No system to manage private/exclusive events
  • Weak community engagement & low participation

Our Solution

  • Centralized web platform for all community events
  • Admin approval system for quality control
  • Public, Private & Paid event management
  • Real-time notifications & feedback system
